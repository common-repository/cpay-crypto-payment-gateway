== Installation ==
 
1. Go to administrative panel of your shop [ wordpress admin page ].
2. In the menu on the left choose the tab “Plugins”, then press “Add a New Plugin”.
3. Type into the search bar “cpay crypto payment gateway” – the plugin will appear at the top of the results list.
4. Press the button “Install”.
5. After the end of installation press the button “Activate”.
6. On the tab WooCommerce go to “Settings” -> “Payments”, in the list of available payment methods choose cpay payments gateway.
7. Activate your account with cpay. Go to this link to create your account - https://cpay-integrations.yolasite.com/
6. Congrates now you can receive payments in crypto currencies across world.
 
== Frequently Asked Questions ==
 
= How do I use this plugin?/ How this plugin works? =
 
Unlike other payments gateways, cpay dosen't need much work from you. After installing and activating your account. When your customer pays through cpay on your checkout page we receive funds and we redirect them to your wallet.
Untill then the order will be "On-Hold". Once you receive the funds you can change the status to order processing/ completed in orders section [wocommerce].
 
= How to uninstall the plugin? =
 
Simply deactivate and delete the plugin.
